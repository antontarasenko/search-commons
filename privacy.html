SearchX Privacy Policy

SearchX does not collect any information about its users or search queries. The only exception is the data collected by Google (SearchX uses Google Custom Search for search). Search requests are sent to Google servers from the user's browser. Also Custom Search makes certain aggregate data available to SearchX (the total number of queries and the list of popular queries). Custom Search does not allow to disown this data.
